FAQ & Help

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the Sign Up button on the homepage and fill in the required details.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
Use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password via your registered email.
How can I update my profile information?
Navigate to the Profile page from your dashboard to update your personal details and preferences.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings > Delete Account and follow the instructions. Note that deletion is permanent.
Who can I contact for support?
You can reach out to our support team via the Contact page or email us at support@pitchbridge.com.
