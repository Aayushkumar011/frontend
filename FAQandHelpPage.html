Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a PitchBridge account?
Click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in your details.
How can I update my profile?
Go to the settings page and choose the appropriate update option like changing username, email, or profile picture.
How do I contact support?
Click the "Contact" link from the sidebar or email us at support@pitchbridge.com. Our team will respond within 24 hours.
Who can access the Pitch Deck and Investor Deck?
Startups can access the Pitch Deck while Investors can view the Investor Deck. The content is role-restricted for privacy and clarity.
Is my data safe on PitchBridge?
Yes, we prioritize security. Your data is stored securely and never shared without your consent. You can adjust privacy settings from your settings page.
What should I do if I forget my password?
Go to the login page and click on “Forgot Password.” You’ll receive an email with a link to reset your password.
